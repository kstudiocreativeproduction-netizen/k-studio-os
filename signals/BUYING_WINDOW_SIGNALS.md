# BUYING WINDOW SIGNALS

## Core Principle

Premium projects rarely appear randomly.

They emerge from:
- organizational tension
- expansion
- visibility pressure
- operational gaps
- strategic transitions
- internal change
- ecosystem momentum

The goal is:
detect buying windows BEFORE procurement begins.

---

# HIGH VALUE BUYING WINDOW SIGNALS

## 1. HIRING SIGNALS

Strong indicators:
- Senior Producer hiring
- Brand Director hiring
- Creative Lead hiring
- Event Producer hiring
- Marketing expansion
- Internal creative scaling
- “Global campaign” language
- “Integrated production” language

organization preparing for execution expansion.

# STRATEGIC INTERPRETATION MODEL

Never ask:
“Do they need production?”

Ask:
“What organizational pressure is forming?”

Because:
organizational pressure creates buying behavior.
